Baked Mac n Cheese

Ingredients

    Noodles; Elbow or shells
    Velveeta cheese
    Milk
    Bread
    Butter

Allergy Waring Contains: Dairy and Gluten

    1. Boli water and add a pinch of salt
    2. Once noodles are mostly cooked place in a baking dish
    3. In a medium sauce pan add 2-3 thin slices of butter, A splash of milk, and 8-12 oz of velveeta
    4. Butter 5-6 slices of bread and cube it
    5. Top noodles with cheese sauce and stir around after that cover the top in bread
    6. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until bread is golden brown
